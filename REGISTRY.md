## Medium Description (Smithery/Glama/mcp.run)

Query current Norwegian statutes (gjeldende lover) and central regulations (gjeldende sentrale forskrifter)
— from Personopplysningsloven and Straffeloven to Aksjeloven —
directly from Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client. Full-text search across
all provisions in both Bokmål and Nynorsk.

Data sourced from api.lovdata.no under NLOD 2.0. Built by Ansvar Systems (ansvar.eu) — part of Ansvar Open Law,
providing structured access to legislation from 100+ jurisdictions worldwide.

This is a research tool, not legal advice. Verify critical citations against official sources (lovdata.no).
